Jansunwai AI
Citizen Portal

Version 1.0

Product Overview

Jansunwai AI is an AI-powered constituency development planning platform that enables citizens to participate directly in the development of their parliamentary constituency.

Instead of functioning as a traditional complaint portal, the platform focuses on collecting development suggestions, analyzing them using Artificial Intelligence, combining them with demographic and infrastructure datasets, and providing MPs with actionable, data-driven recommendations for future development planning.

The Citizen Portal acts as the primary source of verified public demand.

Problem Statement

Today MPs receive thousands of development requests through

Public meetings
Letters
WhatsApp
Social media
Grievance portals
Local representatives

These requests remain scattered across multiple platforms.

There is no intelligent system that can

Understand citizen priorities
Identify recurring development needs
Detect demand hotspots
Compare requests with existing infrastructure
Recommend which development work should be prioritized first

Jansunwai AI solves this problem.
