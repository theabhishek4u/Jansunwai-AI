MP Dashboard (AI Decision Intelligence Platform)

Version: 1.0

Role: Member of Parliament (MP)

Platform: Responsive Web Application

Product Vision

The MP Dashboard is an AI-powered constituency decision intelligence platform that helps Members of Parliament make faster, data-driven, and transparent development decisions.

Instead of manually reviewing thousands of citizen requests, MPs receive AI-generated insights, demand analysis, infrastructure gap reports, budget recommendations, and prioritized development plans.

The dashboard transforms scattered citizen feedback into actionable development strategies.

Primary Goals
Centralize all development suggestions.
Prioritize projects using AI.
Visualize demand hotspots.
Allocate budgets intelligently.
Generate constituency development reports.
Improve transparency and decision-making.

⭐ Winning Feature

To make Jansunwai AI stand out from other hackathon projects, I recommend adding a Constituency Digital Twin. This is an interactive 3D/2D map of the MP's constituency where roads, schools, hospitals, water facilities, and citizen suggestions are visualized in one place. Instead of viewing isolated complaints, the MP sees the entire constituency as a living digital model. Clicking any village instantly reveals infrastructure gaps, AI-generated priority scores, citizen demand trends, and recommended projects. This single feature demonstrates practical AI decision support and creates a memorable demo for judges because it goes beyond complaint management into intelligent constituency planning.
